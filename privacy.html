TruthCart Privacy Policy

TruthCart does not collect or store personal user data.

What we access:

  • Product page title
  • Product URL

What we do NOT collect:

  • Cookies
  • Login credentials
  • Browsing history
  • IP addresses
  • Personal identifiers

TruthCart only analyzes publicly available product discussions from platforms such as Reddit, X (Twitter), and YouTube for the purpose of generating a review authenticity score. All analysis is anonymous and cached temporarily for performance.
